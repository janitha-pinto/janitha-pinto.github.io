Our Story

Home-style Sri Lankan cooking, made for celebrations

Ceylon Spice Catering started in a Melbourne home kitchen, sharing recipes passed down through generations. Today we cater weddings, corporate lunches and festivals across Melbourne — but every curry is still made in small batches, from scratch, the way it's done back home.

  • Fresh-ground spice blends, roasted in-house
  • Vegetarian, vegan & gluten-free options on every menu
  • Fully licensed & insured mobile catering
  • Servicing all of Melbourne metro and surrounds
